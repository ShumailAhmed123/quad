The Quad is a comprehensive, non-partisan resource for all things related to speech on college and university campuses in the United States.

Featuring a first-of-its kind directory of organizations and influencers championing campus free speech, the Quad is also your source for the latest surveys and research about college students and free expression.

This resource is a product of the First Amendment Center at the Freedom Forum Institute. The organization and journalist directories were compiled in partnership with the Fors Marsh Group.

Research

The First Amendment Center at the Freedom Forum Institute invites contributions from various academic disciplines exploring free expression issues on college campuses. Selected papers, law review articles, research results, and other works of scholarship will be published on the Quad website.

Submissions are accepted on a rolling basis. Please send an abstract (no longer than 500 words) to lnott@freedomforum.org, with the words "Research Paper" in the subject line.
